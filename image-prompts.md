## Technical Specifications for All Images

### Aspect Ratios:
- **Hero & Services Headers:** `--ar 16:9` (1920x1080 minimum, 4K preferred)
- **About Portrait:** `--ar 3:4` or `--ar 4:5` (portrait orientation)
- **Contact Header:** `--ar 16:9` (wide landscape)
- **CTA Image:** `--ar 16:9` or `--ar 21:9` (wide format)

### Quality Settings (Midjourney/DALL-E):
- **Resolution:** Add `--ar 16:9 --v 6` for high quality (Midjourney)
- **Style:** Add `--style raw` for more realistic images (Midjourney)
- **Quality:** Use `--q 2` for highest quality (Midjourney)
- **For DALL-E:** Specify "high resolution", "4K", "professional photography"

### Image Parameters:
- **No Text:** Always add `--no text, words, letters, signage, signs, billboards`
- **Color Palette:** Match primary brand colors (adjust in prompts if needed)
- **Mood:** Professional, trustworthy, sophisticated, Swiss elegance
- **Lighting:** Warm, professional, cinematic for hero; natural for portraits

### Color Guidelines:
- **Primary Colors:** Deep blues (#1e3a5f), warm golds (#d4af37), elegant grays
- **Accent:** Rich blues, sophisticated navy, clean whites
- **Atmosphere:** Prestigious, trustworthy, professional, Swiss precision
